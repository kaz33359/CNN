Our SERVICES

AIR FREIGHT

We are well connected to handle the movement of your shipments which require air freight anywhere in the world. We provide dedicated network of ground and air services from collection to delivery. We can also provide exclusive charter of heavy lift aircraft through to smaller requirements.

SEA FREIGHT

Sea Freight operations and management worldwide by our highly experienced personnel with 24 hours support when needed gives you the complete peace of mind that you are safe with us. Cargo handling includes container, both as full load or part load, and where required break bulk cargo to cater for your non-conventional cargo.

HEAVY EQUIPMENTS RENTAL

We provide Cranes, Forklifts, other lifting equipment’s which are fully insured on short- and long-term contract, including operators if required. We cater specialized services of highly experienced Manpower/Rigger for services like offloading/loading including lashing of all type of Equipment’s.

CUSTOMS CLEARANCE / DOCUMENTATION SERVICES

We provide Customs Clearance documentation services in UAE, be it Air, Sea & Land. Our team are industry experts and well versed with all procedures/protocols relating to and related documentation.

WAREHOUSING AND DISTRIBUTION

In UAE, we provide Warehousing (including open yard/closed storage), Yard management and Distribution services at the most competitive rates. With one of the most geographically convenient locations in Hamriyah Free Zone, Sharjah and Jebel Ali Free Zone, Dubai. We provide is fully secure and with hard standing ground, We can help our clients all kinds of cargo including Rigs and Rig equipment’s, Heavy machinery, Palletized cargo, Tubulars, Containers, General Industrial Equipment’s etc.

LAND TRANSPORT

We provide within UAE local and all GCC road freight services on a full load or groupage cargo including all formalities regarding border & customs clearance. As our customer, you will be able to move all types of cargo including out of gauge cargo. We realize the urgency of operations and provide case by case complete support for urgent shipments/cargo which we call as ‘OCC – Operationally Critical Cargo’ With our extensive network of transport providers who are fully insured and operate under maximum safety regulations, we have the ability to access vast fleets of all types trailers, trucks, pick-up vans, including low bed trailers for that special move.

We have a large network and absolutely confident that their cargo is always handled professionally and securely.

DRILLING RIG MOBILIZATION

As drilling operations continue to expand this market, both safety and performance lead conversations in operations meetings. Concerns with regard to the flat time associated with rig mobilization are held paramount by successful operators because these mobilization operations are costly, hazardous, and problematic to manage. Developing a robust HSE reporting system is crucial to MOB operations because of the huge amount of third-party interaction. Engage will lead this process through JSA coordination, HSE documentation training, and leadership coaching with all levels of those involved.

Our Rig Mobilization Teams work with vendors and drilling crews to get you back on bottom. The process starts by developing a formalized bid package for each rig’s required equipment to allow for better cost tracking. Once all vendors are under contract our teams work with well site leaders and engineers to identify a custom critical path for each rig.
